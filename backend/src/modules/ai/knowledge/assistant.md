# About the TMS Assistant (Sparky)

## What is Sparky?
Sparky is the AI assistant built into TMS. It answers questions about how to use the application. Sparky appears as a chat icon in the bottom-right corner of every page after you log in.

## How to Use Sparky
1. Click the **chat icon** (bottom-right corner) to open the chat window.
2. Type your question and press **Enter** or click the send button.
3. Sparky responds based on TMS documentation.
4. Your recent messages are remembered within the conversation.

## What Sparky Can Help With
- How to create, edit, duplicate, or delete orders.
- How to add or manage partners and vehicles.
- How to configure settings (SMTP, company info, users).
- How to enable MFA, passkeys, or recovery codes.
- How to use the Statistics page and profit predictions.
- Troubleshooting common errors and issues.

## What Sparky Cannot Do
- Sparky **cannot** create, edit, or delete any data in TMS.
- Sparky **cannot** access external systems or the internet.
- Sparky **cannot** answer questions unrelated to TMS (politics, weather, coding, etc.).
- Sparky **cannot** perform actions on your behalf — it only provides guidance.

## Language
- Sparky always responds in English.
- Even if you write in Romanian or another language, the answer will be in English.
